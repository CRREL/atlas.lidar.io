Power

ATLAS's power system consists of six large solar panels, providing about 2kWp, and two EFOY fuel cell generators, providing about 160W. During the summer months, the solar supply provides more than enough power for the system. As the sun descends during the shoulder seasons, the EFOY fuel cells take over and provide power into the winter.
